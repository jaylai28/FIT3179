Unemployment Rate doesn't just impact those individuals who are jobless, it adversely affects the disposable income of the families as well as affecting those who are still employed. This will create a cascading effect that ripples through the economy. Here's more information on the unemployment rates in US, and what factors has affected the country's economy.
